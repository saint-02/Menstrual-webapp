Frequently Asked Questions!

What is menstruation?

Menstruation is the monthly shedding of the uterine lining (endometrium) when pregnancy doesn't occur. It results in bleeding from the vagina, 2-7 days.

At what age does menstruation start?

Menstruation typically start between at ages 9 and 15, with an average onset around 12 years old.

How long does a menstrustion last?

A cycle is typically 28 days but can range from 21 to 35 days.

How long does a period last?

Most periods last between 3 and 7 days.

What are the phases of the menstruation?

  1. Menstrual phase
  2. Follicular phase
  3. Ovulation
  4. Luteal phase

What is ovulation?

Ovulation is when an ovary releases an egg. It usually occurs around day 14 of the 28-day cycle.

Can you get pregnant during your periods?

It's unlikely but it's possible, especially with short cycles or irregular periods.

What causes irregular periods?

Stress, hormonal imbalance, weight changes, medication, PCOS, and thyroid issues.

What is a normal amount of blood?

Losing 30-80ml of blood is typical. Changing pads/tampons every 3-4 hours is considered normal.

What is PMS?

Premenstrual Syndrome includes symptoms like mood swings, bloating, fatigue, and irritability before menstrustion.

What is PMDD?

Premenstrual Dysphoric disorder is a severe form of PMS, with extreme emotional and physical symptoms.

How can I relieve period cramps?

Try heat pads, exercise, hydration, gentle massage, or medication like ibuprofen.

should I exercise during my period?

Yes. light exercise like walking or yoga can help relieve symptoms.

What if I miss a period?

It could due to pregnancy, stress, illness, or hormonal imbalances. If it happens often, see a doctor.

What is considered an irregular period?

Cycles shorter than 21 days or longer than 35 days, or missed periods, may be irregular.

Is it normal to have clots during menstruation?

Small clots are normal, but large clots may suggest heavy bleeding (menorrhagi).

What is menorrhagia?

Excessive menstrual bleeding, often requiring change of pad every 1-2 hours.

Can periods affect mood?

Yes. Hormonal changes can cause anxiety, sadness, irritability, or mood swings.

What is spotting?

Light bleeding between periods. It can be due to birth control, ovulation, or health issues.

Is white or clear discharge normal?

Yes, it is part of the healthy cycle. If it changes color or smells bad, see a doctor.

Do all girls get periods at the same age?

No. Genetics, health, and body weight influence when periods begin.

How can I track my period?

Use apps, calendars, or physical trackers. This help notice patterns and irregularities.

What is amenorhea?

The absence of menstruation. It may be primary (never started) or secondary (stopped for 3+ months).

Is it safe to use tampons or menstrual cycle?

Yes, when used correctly and changed regularly.

Can I swim during my period?

Yes. Use a tampon or menstrual cup to avoid leakage.

Can I get my period twice a day?

Yes, especially if your cycle is shorter than 28 days.

What is implantation bleeding?

Light spotting that occurs when a fertilized egg attaches to uterus. It maybe mistaken for a light period.

Do birth control pills affect menstruation?

Yes. They can make periods lighter, more regular, or stop them all together.

Is it normal to feel tired during perio?

Yes. Hormones, blood loss, and cramps, can cause fstigue.

When should I see a doctor about my periods?

    If it:
  1. Very painful
  2. Heavy bleeding
  3. Irregular often
  4. Missed for months
  5. Causing emotional distress

What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome affects hormones and can lead to irregular periods, acne, and fertility issues.

Can stress affect periods?

Yes. Stress hormones can delay or stop ovulation.

Can diet affect menstruation?

Yes. Extreme dieting or low body weight can delay or stop periods.

Do all woman get PMS?

Not all. Around 75-90%, have mild symptoms; 5-10% have severe PMDD.

Is it okay to fast during your periods?

Generally, yes - but listen to your body. If you feel dizzy or weak, eat and hydrate.

Do periods sync between women?

There's no scientific proof, but anecdotal experience experiences it can happen.

Can exercise delay my periods?

Excessive training (e.g.., athletes) may suppress menstruation due to low body fat.

What is a hormonal IUD and how does it affect periods?

It's birth control device. It can reduce or stop periods over time.
